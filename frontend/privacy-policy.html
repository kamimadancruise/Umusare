Privacy Policy

How Umusare handles data.

Last updated: 2026. This draft placeholder should be reviewed by a Rwandan legal professional before launch.

01 Data collected 02 Client data 03 Driver data 04 Use of data 05 Public visibility 06 Sharing
01

What Data Umusare Collects

Umusare may collect information needed to operate the platform, verify drivers, process bookings, manage reviews, and review safety reports.

02

Client Data

  • Name
  • Phone number
  • Email
  • Booking details
  • Reports and reviews
03

Driver Data

  • Name, age, gender, and contact details
  • Profile photo
  • Driver's licence information
  • ID or passport information
  • Education proof
  • Booking history
  • Reviews and reports
04

How Data Is Used

Data may be used to operate the platform, verify drivers, process bookings, support safety and incident review, and improve service quality.

05

Public and Private Information

Private documents should not be displayed publicly. Driver profile information such as name, photo, languages, experience, gender, rating, and location may be visible to clients.

06

Sharing and Authorities

Umusare does not sell user personal data to third parties. Information may be shared with lawful authorities where legally required.
