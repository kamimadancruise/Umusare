Safety Policy

Trip safety expectations.

Last updated: 2026. This draft placeholder should be reviewed by a Rwandan legal professional before launch.

01 Client safety02 Driver conduct03 Property04 Reports
01

Client Safety Before a Trip

Clients should verify that the driver's face matches the profile photo before starting the trip. The driver operates the client's own vehicle, and clients should check their own vehicle insurance policy regarding third-party drivers.

Clients may cancel before the trip starts if they feel unsafe.

02

Driver Safety Duties

Drivers must follow Rwandan traffic laws and must not drive under the influence of alcohol, drugs, or any impairing substance.

03

Vehicle and Property Rules

Drivers must not smoke in the client's vehicle and must not search through, remove, or misuse client property.

04

Safety Reports and Emergencies

Clients and drivers can report safety issues through Umusare. For immediate danger, users should contact emergency services first.
