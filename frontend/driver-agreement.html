01

Driver Eligibility

Drivers must provide accurate identity, contact, licence, experience, language, vehicle-skill, and location information before their profile may be reviewed by Umusare.

02

Independent Contractor Relationship

Drivers using Umusare are independent service providers. Umusare is a technology and marketplace platform and does not employ drivers, own vehicles, or operate transport services.

03

Required Documents

  • National ID or passport
  • Driver's licence
  • Proof of secondary education
  • Recent profile photo
  • Police Clearance or Criminal Record Certificate
04

Document Verification

Umusare may review submitted documents before a driver profile becomes visible. Private document images should not be displayed publicly.

Drivers may be required to provide a recent Police Clearance or Criminal Record Certificate as part of Umusare's safety verification process.

05

Driver Obligations Before Every Trip

Drivers should confirm the booking details, client pickup point, destination, client vehicle type, and any special instructions before starting a trip.

06

Driver Conduct During Trips

Drivers must obey Rwandan traffic laws, drive safely, respect client property, avoid smoking in the client vehicle, and never drive under the influence of alcohol, drugs, or impairing substances.

Prohibited conduct includes theft, harassment, searching client property, misrepresenting skills or experience, unsafe driving, and allowing an unapproved person to perform the trip.

07

Accidents, Incidents, Theft, and Security

Drivers must promptly report accidents, safety concerns, misconduct, theft or missing property, payment disputes, wrong booking details, and other serious issues through Umusare. Emergency situations should be handled through emergency services first.

08

Ratings, Reviews, Suspension, and Termination

Clients may submit ratings and reviews after completed trips. Umusare may suspend, hide, or remove a driver profile for misconduct, unsafe behavior, false information, unresolved incidents, or abuse of the platform.

09

10

Acceptance of Agreement

By applying as a driver or using the driver dashboard, a driver confirms that the information submitted is accurate and agrees to follow Umusare platform rules.
