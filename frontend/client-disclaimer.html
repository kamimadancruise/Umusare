Umusare's Role

Umusare helps clients find verified independent drivers. Umusare does not guarantee that every trip will be risk-free.

02

Your Vehicle and Insurance

The driver will drive the client's own vehicle. The client should check insurance coverage for third-party drivers and should not begin a trip if they feel unsafe.

03

Identity and Disputes

The client should confirm the driver's identity before starting. Umusare may assist with platform records and reports, but disputes between client and driver may remain between those parties.
