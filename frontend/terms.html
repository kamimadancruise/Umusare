01

Marketplace and Technology Platform

Umusare is a marketplace and technology platform only. It connects clients with independent drivers and does not employ drivers.

Umusare does not own, operate, or maintain vehicles. Drivers operate the client's own vehicle.

02

Client and Driver Responsibilities

Clients are responsible for providing accurate booking information, pickup details, destinations, and vehicle information. Drivers are responsible for safe driving and obeying Rwandan traffic laws.

03

Bookings, Cancellations, Reviews, and Reports

Bookings, cancellations, reviews, complaints, and reports are handled through the Umusare platform using front-end workflows until a backend is connected.

04

Misconduct and Account Action

Umusare may suspend or remove users for misconduct, false information, unsafe behavior, unresolved safety reports, or abuse of the platform.

05

Governing Law

These draft terms are intended to be governed by the laws of the Republic of Rwanda once reviewed and finalized by legal counsel.
