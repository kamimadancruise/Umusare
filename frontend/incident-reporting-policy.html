Incident Reporting Policy

How safety reports are reviewed.

Last updated: 2026. This draft placeholder should be reviewed by a Rwandan legal professional before launch.

01 What can be reported02 Review process03 Statuses04 Information needed05 Emergencies
01

What Can Be Reported

  • Safety concern
  • Accident
  • Misconduct
  • Theft or missing property
  • Wrong driver arrived
  • Vehicle issue
  • Payment dispute
  • Client misconduct
  • Driver misconduct
  • Other
02

How Reports Are Reviewed

Umusare may review platform records, booking details, submitted descriptions, and uploaded evidence placeholders. Serious reports may lead to temporary driver profile hiding or suspension while review continues.

03

Report Statuses

New
Report received.
Under Review
Staff are checking details.
Awaiting Response
More information is needed.
Resolved
Review has been closed.
Escalated
Higher priority follow-up is required.
04

Information Users Should Provide

  • Booking ID
  • Driver or client name
  • Date of incident
  • Description
  • Evidence/photo if available
05

Emergency Situations

Emergency situations should be reported to emergency services first. Umusare reports are for platform safety review and follow-up.
